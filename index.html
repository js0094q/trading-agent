Trading Agent Starter

Two lightweight agents: Research (daily prep) and Risk/Sizing (order-ready sizes). Bring your own data and signals; the runner enforces guardrails and writes artifacts.

Commands

./agent_runner.py research --stub
./agent_runner.py sizing --equity 100000
  • Research: validates inputs and can emit stub outputs.
  • Sizing: strict stop-distance checks, clamps to risk caps.

Inputs

  • inputs/preferences.json
  • inputs/universe.txt
  • inputs/strategy_spec.md
  • artifacts/signals/trade_plans.json
  • Optional: artifacts/signals/screener.json

Outputs

  • artifacts/research/daily_brief.md
  • artifacts/research/watchlist.json
  • artifacts/sizing/order_sheet.json
  • artifacts/sizing/risk_checklist.md
